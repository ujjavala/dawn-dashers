How Dawn Dashers Works

Adventure ObjectiveYou are a dawn runner in the Southern Hemisphere. Cross Outback, Bushland, Servo, Coastline, and Tasmania to collect all 7 shards and make the sun rise again.
Puzzle Type 1: Treasure ChestFind a chest in the run, solve its treasure puzzle, and earn food supplies only.
Puzzle Type 2: Heart RevivalIf hearts hit 0, your first wipe in that level opens a heart revival challenge. Solve all level core puzzles to restore all 3 hearts.
Puzzle Type 3: Next-Level UnlockAfter clearing a level, solve the unlock puzzle to proceed to the next terrain.
Heart RulesYou always have 3 hearts. Each new level resets to 3. Hearts do not carry to next level. Revival can happen only once per level; if hearts hit 0 again in that level, game over.
Falling ObjectsCollect shards and positive score tokens. Avoid bombs and negative score tokens.
ScoringScore comes from score tokens and level clear bonus.
Energy and FoodMove, jump, and slide consume energy. Buy food with score and it auto-uses when energy is low.
Character PairsEach level gives 2 dashers: one fast and one slow. Special dashers unlock by collecting 3 treasure chests in one run for that level.
ControlsArrows/WASD move, Up/Space jump, Down slide, F opens Food Cart.
